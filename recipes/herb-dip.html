Herb Dip

Serving Size: 2 tablespoons, 12 servings 28 mg sodium per serving Ingredients: 1/2 cup skim milk 1/2 block of silken tofu 1 cup low fat cottage cheese 2 tablespoons chopped parsley 2 tablespoons chopped chives 1/2 teaspoon dried basil 1/8 teaspoon curry powder (optional) 1/8 teaspoon paprika 1 small garlic clove, minced Preparation: 1. Puree all ingredients in blender until smooth. 2. Serve with crisp raw vegetables.
